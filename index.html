About:

I love to collaborate and work on wonderful projects. I began my filmmaking career in Newfoundland, where I received a diploma and had the chance to work in some of the most beautiful locations.

In school, I was drawn to the camera department and learned as much as I could about the equipment. I'm currently located in Kitchener, Ontario and actively seek work in the neighbouring areas (Toronto, London, Hamilton, Mississauga, etc). I'm excited by the explosive growth of the film industry in these areas.

Whether you're a creative solo artist or an organization of story-tellers, I look forward to working with you.
